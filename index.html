Welcome to Memphis Ruby!

The Memphis Ruby Users Group is a software group interested in topics related to the programming language, Ruby. We have monthly meetups at various locations throughout the Greater Memphis Area. We usually have two presentations each month, given by local practicioners. Our members and topics range from novice to expert, and we welcome newcomers!
