Memphis Ruby

The Memphis Ruby Users Group is a software group interested in sharing and promoting topics related to the Ruby programming language. We have monthly meetups at various locations throughout the Greater Memphis Area. We have presentations each month given by a local practitioner. Our members and topics range from novice to expert, and we always welcome newcomers.
